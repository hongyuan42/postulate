About

Founded in 2016 by Dr Hongyuan Liu (Hong), Postulate develop engineering solutions and help clients transform concepts into viable products by leveraging our expertise in electronics and algorithms, along with our know-how in diagnostic instrumentation.


Hong holds an MPhil in Mixed-Signal Electronics and a PhD in Biosensors. With a background in electronics and algorithms, his expertise extends to data science and computer vision. He has over 15 years of experience in developing technologies and electronic systems for in vitro diagnostics and health monitoring.

Expertise & Offerings

  • Electronic System

    We design system architectures, hardware, and software for integrated sensing, fluidics management, thermal control, and other instrument functionalities.

  • Data & Image Processing

    We develop algorithms, including computer vision and deep learning, for feature extraction and analysis of data and images.

  • Instrument & Prototype

    We build modular, scalable instrument prototypes, emphasizing usability and user-configurability.
